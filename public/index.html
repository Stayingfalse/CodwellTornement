🕵️ How the Tournament Works

Overview

This is a round-robin Codenames tournament. Every player is paired with every other player across multiple rounds, so everyone gets to work with different teammates and face different opponents.

Match Format — Two Games Per Match

Each match-up consists of two games. After Game 1 the roles are swapped — spymasters become guessers and vice versa — so each player tries both roles against the same opponents.

  • Game 1 — Blue Spymaster A & Blue Guesser B vs Red Spymaster C & Red Guesser D
  • Game 2 — Roles swapped: Blue Spymaster B & Blue Guesser A vs Red Spymaster D & Red Guesser C

Scoring

Points are awarded per game based on how convincingly the winning team won:

  • Assassin word hit — The guessing team accidentally picks the assassin, immediately losing. The winning team earns a large bonus.
  • Cards remaining — A standard win where the winning team clears their cards first. More cards left on the board for the opposing team = more points for the winners.
  • The losing team may also receive a small consolation score depending on how close the game was.

Rounds

Matches within a round all run simultaneously in their own Discord threads. A round ends once every match has both games completed (or is skipped by an admin). The next round is then allocated automatically.
